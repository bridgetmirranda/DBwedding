I found the one my heart loves

Song of Solomon 3:4

Our mutual friends thought we'd be perfect together, even though we never spoke to each other during our high school and college years. But several years later we happened to be part of our friends' wedding party, and saw each other in a new light. Fast forward eight years, our love and commitment for one another is so much stronger. And we can't wait to make it official!

He proposed

May 15, 2018

Dacquain proposed to me on my birthday!! And yes I was surprised! We had reservations at Ray's on a River which has a beautiful scenic route along the Chattahoochee River. We had enough time to take a stroll and just enjoy one another. I was so happy in that moment. Before we walked back to the restaurant, he asked a nice couple to take a picture of us. The couple did a good job directing me where to position for this picture..LOL. When I turned around, I saw the little Tiffany Blue Box! YAAYYYY! He asked me to be his wife, and I said 'YES'!
